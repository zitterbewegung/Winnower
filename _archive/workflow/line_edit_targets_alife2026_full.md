# Line-edit targets for the ALIFE full paper

These are the highest-value places to improve if time is limited.

## Priority 1
- abstract
- the first 3 paragraphs of the introduction
- the paragraph immediately before Figure 1
- the short interpretation paragraphs after Theorem 1 and Theorem 2

## Priority 2
- the first paragraph of `Experiments`
- the opening paragraphs of `Cross-dimensional stabilization` and `Why the complexity penalty matters`
- the first paragraph of `Discussion`
- the first paragraph of `Conclusion`

## Priority 3
- figure captions
- repeated uses of phrases like:
  - “the contribution is”
  - “the framework”
  - “the model family”
  - “global selector”
  - “structured residual”

## Style problems to watch for
- sentences carrying more than one main idea
- repeated disclaimers in slightly different wording
- abstract nouns where a direct verb would be clearer
- theorem motivation that sounds administrative rather than explanatory
- paragraphs that describe the paper instead of advancing the argument
