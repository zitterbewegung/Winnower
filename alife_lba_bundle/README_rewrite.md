2. physicaD_knuth_rewrite.tex / .pdf
   - Physica D submission-style draft.

Rewriting choices applied from the mathematical-writing notes:
- Open with the phenomenon rather than a theorem.
- Keep theorem statements motivated and self-contained.
- Favor short, direct sentences with running commentary around formulas.
- Reduce repeated robotic phrasing and theorem-first exposition.
- Use examples and visual summaries early.

Author metadata is left in editable draft form.
