Cellular-automaton cube

A CA run rendered as a rotating 3D voxel cube — either a 2D rule stacked along its time axis (a spacetime cube), or a true 3D totalistic rule from ca3d.py. Drag to orbit, scroll to zoom.

…

Voxels are live cells. In spacetime mode the vertical axis is time (bottom = initial condition, top = final step) and colour tracks the time step; in volume mode colour tracks height. Everything runs locally in your browser — no data leaves the page.
